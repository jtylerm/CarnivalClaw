Instructions


- Install the iOS app


- Choose "Play As Guest"


- Aim device's camera at the orientation image (don't let your finger cover camera at all)


- Tap corresponding buttons to shoot claw at top and bottom rows


- Collect all rupees and bags. Avoid bombs!


- Item points: Green = 2; Blue = 10, Red = 20; Bag = 50; Bomb = -20


- Username is generated for you


- High scores will be displayed in table on full website version
