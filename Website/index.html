Tyler Mayberry - Arts Studies Senior

Carnival Claw is an augmented reality video game where users are meant to tap corresponding buttons to grab items from the top or bottom rows with the claw. Grab all the Rupees and Bags while avoiding the Bombs for your best score! I made all art with Adobe Illustrator and Blender, I coded and created the app with Unity, and I wrote the website for the game.

Carnival Claw is my submission for the 2017 Code + Art Visualization Contest.
